Computational Platforms Comparison

The idea is to see if the computational publishing platforms could be a presentation platforms for authors to create publication from LoD collections.

Currently most digital heritage archives are obscured by database models of presentation.

Give example and make demo:

  • First as a dummy doc in ADA to show what is meant

  • Then in one of the platforms below: probably Curvenote, maybe Jupyter Book?

  • Document and present as computational publication with view on how could be implemented and integrated with the ADA Pipeline.
